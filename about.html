Our Story

Built in Egypt, Trusted Across the Region

Vortex started as a single idea: that Egyptian creative talent deserves a platform that competes globally. Led by Ahmed Abo Shady, our team has grown to include senior designers, marketing managers, AI video specialists, web developers, and brand identity artists.

We've built 8 live websites and e-commerce stores — from Five Hills Real Estate to Juyub and Funny Bunny — launched 3 mobile apps, and run campaigns that generated over 1.4 million views with growth peaks of 1,895%. We don't just design — we deliver measurable results.
