Marketing & Sales Strategy

Client Acquisition Roadmap

An actionable tactical playbook designed for Hadeer Mustafa (Marketing Manager) to generate high-end B2B leads and manage the client onboarding journey to successful contract closures.

Part 1

Lead Generation Channels

Website Portfolio

Our portfolio is already live on our website, showcasing detailed design case studies for branding, packaging, and exhibition design to demonstrate our visual systems and strategic results.

AI Media & Reels

Creating short, viral visual hooks demonstrating AI video generation and premium motion design capabilities. Spearheaded by Ahmed Nasr to showcase our state-of-the-art tech.

B2B LinkedIn Outreach

Direct outreach targeting Real Estate Marketing Directors and Luxury Brand Founders. Sending highly personalized value propositions based on their current visual pain points.

Facebook Organic Leads

Ahmed Abo Shady will monitor and track target Facebook groups to identify businesses searching for website development, marketing agencies, packaging designs, or exhibition setups to acquire organic B2B leads.

Part 2

The Client Journey

The step-by-step pipeline from first contact to signed contract.

Step 01

Lead Qualification

Filter incoming requests from our brief form. Evaluate budget fit, timeline alignment, and scope complexity.

Step 02

Discovery Call

A 15-minute alignment call with the client to understand goals, pain points, and detail project requirements.

Step 03

Proposal & Moodboard

A internal sync with Ahmed Nasr (Creative Director) to draft a creative concept pitch deck containing scope, deliverables, and financial terms.

Step 04

Negotiation

Aligning details, revisions limits, and scope tweaks with the client to achieve mutual terms agreement.

Step 05

Contracting & Payment

Signing the official agreement. Standard terms: 50% deposit, 30% on initial reviews, 20% on final files delivery.

Step 06

Handover to Operations

Officially introducing the client to Panseih (Team Manager) to initiate timeline tracking and design tasks.
