Policies

Rules & Compensation

Team Rules & Communication

We operate on a hybrid model focusing on deliverables. Slack is our exclusive communication channel, and task tracking is managed on Odoo Project. Weekly syncs occur every Monday at 4:00 PM to review pipeline tasks. Strict NDA on active work.

Payroll Schedule

Base salaries are systematically paid out between the 1st and 5th of each calendar month via banking transfers or active wallets as chosen by team members.

Bonus & Commissions

Project-based bonuses are awarded for rapid deliveries and zero-error project approvals. Referral commissions are paid to any member who introduces new paying clients to the agency.
